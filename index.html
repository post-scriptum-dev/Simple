What is intermittent fasting?

Intermittent fasting is not a diet but an eating pattern based on following a meals schedule. It doesn’t involve restriction in the intake of calories so you don’t have to refuse your favorite foods. In simple terms, intermittent fasting changes when you eat, not what you eat.

Why it works:

- It is natural for your body

- It is easy to keep up with so you don’t drop it

- It is scientifically proven to drive energy and mindfullness

- It is not demanding to your family, social and work habits and routine
